Detailed Comments In this paper, the authors present their winning solution for KDD CUP 2015 on dropout prediction in a MOOC platform.

The organization of this paper needs significant improvement before being published. Based on it current shape, it looks like a competition report or workshop paper.

Furthermore, the motivation of the problem is not described well. Regarding the proposed solution, the authors just list all of hand-crafted or auto-generated features as well as the base classifiers used for ensemble learning for the KDD CUP 2015 competition. No explanations are presented, e.g., why the proposed features or ensemble strategies are useful for the dropout prediction task for online education? In addition, the proposed solution looks dataset-specific (KDD CUP 2015). Whether the proposed system/solution can be applied to a dropout prediction task in another MOOC platform is unknown. Therefore, knowledge that can be learned from this paper is limited.
